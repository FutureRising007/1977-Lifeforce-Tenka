******HI THESE ARE THE CONTROLS FOR THE GAME******

1. Jump - Space Bar
2. Move Forward - W
3. Move Backward - s
4. Move Left - A
5. Move Right - D
6. Fire Gun - Left Mouse Button
7. Crouch - C
8. Sprint - Shift + W

#Reload takes place automatically when you're mag is finished. All you need to do is to collect the mag from the pickup
